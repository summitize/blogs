Job interviews can be nerve-wracking, but with ChatGPT as your interview buddy, you can practice and prepare like never before. This AI tool simulates real interview scenarios to help you ace your next opportunity.

Practice Common Interview Questions

ChatGPT can generate a wide range of questions based on your industry and role. From behavioral to technical questions, it covers all bases.

Get Instant Feedback

After answering, ChatGPT provides constructive feedback on your responses, helping you improve your communication skills and confidence.

Tips for Using ChatGPT in Interview Prep

  • Specify your job title and industry for tailored questions
  • Practice in a quiet environment
  • Review feedback and iterate
